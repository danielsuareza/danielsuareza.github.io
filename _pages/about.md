I am a first-year Operations Research PhD student at Georgia Tech ISyE, working with Prof. Devansh Jalota. My research lies in the intersection of optimization, learning, and economics with a focus on sequential decision-making under uncertainty and reinforcement learning. Currently focused on online learning for problems arising in strategic electricity markets.

Before Georgia Tech, I completed a BS at Rice University with majors in Operations Research and Mathematical Economics, and a minor in Financial Computation & Modeling.

Born and raised in Ecuador 🇪🇨. FC Barcelona fan. Lifter, incoming triathlete.
